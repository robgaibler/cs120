Trip Info

Every year, a group of my friends travel to Lake Winnipesaukee in New Hampshire. We hike in the White Mountains, go into the small, quaint towns to vitage shop, and rent a pontoon boat for a day. It is my favorit weekend each year! This year, we have a bigger group of friends going, and I cannot wait to show them all the magic of the lake!

Favorite Restaurant and Food

My favorite restaurant is Area Four. I get an espresso over ice (splash of milk) every morning and sometimes splurge on a chocolate croissant. Their sour dough crost pizza is to die for - I defintiely recommend the mushroom pizza.

My favorite food is buffalo wings with blue cheese (not ranch)!
